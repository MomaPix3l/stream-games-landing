Guide

How to Use Your Wonderland Cards

Wonderland cards let you interact with the stream in real time. Here’s the actual flow:

  • •Earn packs/cards during streams via drops, events, and support moments.
  • •Open your Card Inventory to view your collection and packs.
  • • Log in with your Twitch name + Wonderland PIN to access your inventory securely.
  • • If you forget your PIN, type !wonderland in chat to receive it by DM.
  • •Open packs in the UI to reveal new cards.
  • •Play cards from the UI to trigger effects when available.

Mini-Games

Chat-Powered Games

Game
DIRTY

A word game that tests how twisted your mind is. You get up to three clues to guess the secret word. Correct guesses earn letters from D I R T Y; wrong guesses cost you letters. First to spell it wins.

Game (By Request)
Yahtzee — Chat Edition

Classic Yahtzee, controlled by chat. Roll up to three times, keep dice, and lock scoring categories. It’s disabled by default and enabled on request during the stream.
